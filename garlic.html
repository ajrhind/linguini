Garlic Bread

save
share


Difficulty: Easy
Time: 15 mins
Rating: 5/5

Ingredients

  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 150g salted butter
  • 1 Tbsn chopped parsley
  • 1 full loaf (ciabatta reccomended)

Method

  1. Soften butter (do not melt!), and preheat oven to 180 degrees on grill.

    Tip: cut the butter into small cubes to let it soften easier.
  2. Cut the garlic as small as possible
  3. Mix the butter, garlic and parsely together
  4. Cut the bread into 2cm slices, spread butter on one side
  5. Place onto baking tray butter side up, and cook for 10 mins or until golden
