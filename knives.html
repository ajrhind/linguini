Difficulty: Easy
Time: 5 mins
Questions: 4

  • When using a knife, you have to make sure that it is sharp. A blunt knife is more dangerous than a sharp one. This is because when you have to cut through something with a blunt knife, you have to put far more pressure into it, and you could slip and cut yourself. Since the knife will be dragging through your flesh instead of cutting, it will leave the wound jagged and make it harder to heal.

  • when washing, NEVER use a scrubbing brush, and never put it in the dishwasher. The knife should be cleaned with a soft wet cloth and hot water with a tiny amount of dish soap.
